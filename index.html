Features

Payroll

Our solution automates the entire payroll process, from calculating salaries and taxes to generating pay slips. With accurate and timely payroll processing, you can ensure your employees are paid correctly and on time.

Attendance

Our system allows employees to mark their attendance digitally via app or web portal, while managers can easily monitor attendance records, track leave balances, and generate insightful reports.

App Support

With our dedicated app support, your workforce can stay connected, manage their profiles, and perform essential tasks like attendance, leaves, exit pass, anytime anywhere. Our app also have advance features like biometric login, single device locking, etc.

Recruitment & Retirement

Simplify the hiring process, streamline retirements, and ensure a smooth transition for both incoming and outgoing employees.

Compliance

Our HRMS software is equipped with robust compliance management features, helping you navigate complex employment laws and regulations.

Asset Management

Job Management
